Camera Tethers for GoPro

$8.99

Prevent losing your GoPro camera in extreme conditions by attaching these adhesive anchors and camera tethers to a variety of surfaces, such as helmets, boats, and surfboards. Simply attach the adhesive anchors to your gear, then secure the camera tethers to the camera housing. If your GoPro comes loose, the tethers add an extra layer of security to keep it attached to your gear. This set includes 5 x camera tethers and 5 x adhesive anchors with industrial strength 3M™ waterproof adhesive for maximum holding strength.

Features

  • Secure your action camera in extreme conditions with these adhesive anchors.
  • Compatible with all GoPro cameras.
  • Includes 5 x adhesive anchors and 5 x Camera Tethers.
  • Strong 3M™ waterproof adhesive for maximum holding strength.
