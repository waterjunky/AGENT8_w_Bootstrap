About AGENT 8

Founded in California in 2015, AGENT 8 is an environmentally minded company committed to offering high quality, long lasting sports and life accessories at reasonable prices, while giving back and supporting environmental causes.

Giving Back

For every AGENT 8 accessory for GoPro and Forever Straw sold, we pledge to donate a portion of proceeds to support environmental causes. So far, donations have been made to Americares; to support worldwide medical aid, disaster relief and recovery, and 1% For The Planet; to support environmentally focused nonprofit organizations. We believe our focus on quality and eco-minded materials will lead to longer lasting products, keeping them out of landfills as well as reducing the use of plastic.

Our Humble Beginnings

We’re a father and son operation, just two nature loving guys that love to be active outdoors. When we designed The Pouch, simply to protect our own action cameras, we didn’t imagine the success it has had. It is with that success that we will continue to design, produce, and source more life and sports accessories that make sense, one product at a time.
